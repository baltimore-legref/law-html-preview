.01 Restatement and Amendment of Area 18 – Middle/East/South.

.02 Parking restrictions in Area 18 – Middle/East/South

A. The PABC shall post and enforce the following parking restrictions:

"Two-hour parking limit, Monday through Friday, from 7:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m."

B. Area 18 permit holders will not be subject to the restrictions in A. of this Regulation.

.03 Special Permit Eligibility.

The PABC may not issue special permits in Area 18.

.04 Permit Limits for Residents.

A dwelling unit in Area 18 may apply for:

A. Up to four vehicle permits; and

B. One visitor permit.
