Code of Baltimore Regulations Annotated

.10 Presence of a Person or Animal During Abatement.

A. An owner or operator shall inform an occupant or tenant of health hazards to a person or animal associated with lead abatement.

B. If the property is a rental dwelling, an owner or operator shall:

(1) Immediately secure a temporary lead-safe or lead-free residence for the tenant; and

(2) Pay the tenants' reasonable relocation expenses until the Commissioner verifies that abatement is completed.

C. A tenant may not return to the property until abatement is completed in accordance with federal and state requirements and this regulation.
