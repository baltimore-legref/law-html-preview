Code of Baltimore Regulations Annotated

.01 Restatement and Amendment of Area 18 – Middle/East/South.

The PABC restates and amends RPP Area 18 – Middle/East/South on the following block faces or portions of block faces:

A. 400 block of North Castle Street (both sides, both sides posted);

B. 500 block of North Castle Street (both sides, both sides posted);

C. 600 block of North Castle Street (both sides, both sides posted);

D. 400 block of North Chapel Street (both sides);

E. 400 block of North Chester Street (both sides, both sides posted);

F. 500 block of North Chester Street (both sides, both sides posted);

G. 200 North Chester Street (both sides, both sides posted);

H. 400 block of North Collington Avenue (both sides, both sides posted);

I. 500 block of North Collington Avenue (both sides, both sides posted);

J. 700 block of North Collington Avenue (both sides, both sides posted);

K. 800 block of North Collington Avenue (odd side, odd side posted);

L. 1900 block of North Jefferson Street (odd side, odd side posted);

M. 2000 block of Jefferson Street (both sides, both sides posted);

N. 2100 block of Jefferson Street (both sides, both sides posted);

O. 2200 block of Jefferson Street (both sides, both sides posted);

P. 2000 block of McElderry Street (both sides, both sides posted);

Q. 2200 block of McElderry Street (both sides, both sides posted);

R. 1900 block of Orleans Street (both sides, both sides posted);

S. 2000 block of Orleans Street (both sides, both sides posted);

T. 2100 block of Orleans Street (both sides, both sides posted);

U. 500 block of North Patterson Park (both sides, both sides posted);

V. 400 block of North Washington Street (both sides, both sides posted);

W. 500 block of North Washington Street (odd side, odd side posted);

X. 400 block of North Wolfe Street (odd side, odd side posted);

Y. 600 North Chester Street (no sign posted); and

Z. 2000 block of E. Fayette Street (even side, even side posted).
