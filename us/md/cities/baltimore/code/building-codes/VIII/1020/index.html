A For requirements for occupancies in Group I-2, see §§ 407.2 and 407.3.

B For a reduction in the fire-resistance rating for occupancies in Group I-3, see § 408.8.

D Group R-3 and R-4 buildings equipped throughout with an automatic sprinkler system in accordance with § 903.3.1.3. See § 903.2.8 for occupancies where automatic sprinkler systems are permitted in accordance with § 903.3.1.3.

E R-occupancy buildings equipped with a single exit in accordance with § 1006.3.4.6.
