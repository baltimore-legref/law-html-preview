Baltimore City Code

§ 3-11. Storage.

Materials and products that are buoyant, flammable, or explosive, or that in times of flooding could be injurious to human, animal, or plant life may not be stored below the flood-protection elevation.

(Ord. 21-020; Ord. 14-208; Ord. 11-516; Ord. 88-188.)
