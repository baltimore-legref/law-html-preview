§ 1-107. Person.

(a) In general.

"Person" means:

(1) an individual;

(2) a receiver, trustee, guardian, personal representative, fiduciary, or representative of any kind; or

(3) a partnership, firm, association, corporation, or other entity of any kind.

(b) Exclusion.

"Person" does not include, except as otherwise expressly provided, a governmental entity or an instrumentality or unit of a governmental entity.

(Ord. 21-017.)
