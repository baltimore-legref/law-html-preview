§ 10-9. Appeals.

(a) Administrative appeal.

Within 30 days of the mailing of a notice of assessment, the owner of the property on which the assessment is levied may appeal the assessment to the Board of Municipal and Zoning Appeals in accordance with the City Charter.

(b) Judicial and appellate review.

(1) A person aggrieved by a decision of the Board of Municipal and Zoning Appeals under this section may seek judicial review of that decision by petition to the Circuit Court for Baltimore City in accordance with the Maryland Rules of Procedure.

(2) A party to the judicial review may appeal the court's final judgment to the Appellate Court of Maryland in accordance with the Maryland Rules of Procedure.

(c) Relation back.

If, on appeal, an assessment is sustained in whole or in part, the lien relates back to the time the work was completed.

(Ord. 24-348 Section 1; Ord. 15-435; Ord. 00-010; Ord. 89-290; Ord. 76-139; Ord. 56-644; Ord. 48-319; Ord. 21-573.)
