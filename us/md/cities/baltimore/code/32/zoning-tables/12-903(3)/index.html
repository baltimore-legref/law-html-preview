Table 12-903(3) Key Highway Waterfront Area

Area Height Limitations
1

Building height may not exceed 290 feet above Mean Low Tide. Building coverage in this area may not exceed the following percentages of the existing aggregate of those areas:

(a) Grade to El. 58 feet: 65%.

(b) El. 58 feet to El. 150 feet: 30%.

(c) El. 150 feet to El. 390 feet: 16%.

Maximum height of El. 58 feet in the area north of the eastward extension of Grindall Street and Key Highway.

No structure located between El. 150 feet and El. 390 feet shall exceed 14,000 square feet in area nor be located less than 100 feet from any structure located between El. 150 feet and El. 390 feet.

Rooftop mechanical equipment may exceed such a maximum permitted elevations, as approved by DHCD.
