Baltimore City Code

§ 2B-37. Master leasing affordable units.

(a) In general.

The City or designated housing providers may enter master lease agreements to manage affordable units developed to comply with § 2B-21(b)(2) {"Inclusionary requirements."} of this article.

(b) Designated housing providers.

The Commissioner may designate housing providers to manage and rent affordable units in accordance with the regulations and procedures adopted by the Commissioner.

(Ord. 24-308 Section 1.)
